Information Collection and Use

Stardew Pocket Guide is designed to be a simple, offline-first companion app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information.

All data related to your in-game progress, such as checklists for the Community Center, tracked fish, or villager preferences, is stored locally on your device only. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

Log Data

This data is collected anonymously and is used solely for the purpose of identifying and resolving bugs to improve the application.
